Featured Projects & Success Stories

At Jorara, we turn agricultural challenges into growth opportunities. Our projects have helped Chilean growers and exporters access new technologies, increase profitability, and consolidate their leadership in the fruit sector.

DESH System in Cherries

We pioneered the introduction and validation of the Chilean DESH training system.

Results:

  • Better size and export quality
  • Efficient harvest and cost reduction

Innovation in Plastic Covers
