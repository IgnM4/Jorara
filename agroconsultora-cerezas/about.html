About Us

Jorara Agricultural Consulting was born in the heart of Chilean orchards with the mission to transform national fruit production through innovation and technical expertise.

For over 23 years, we have been guiding growers, companies, and exporters on their path to excellence and international competitiveness.

Our Story

Led by Ricardo Miño, an agricultural engineer and pioneer in training systems, we were the first to validate and adapt the DESH system for cherries in Chile, applying scientific research to real-world farming challenges. Our team has traveled across all major production regions, building strong relationships with growers, companies, and innovation entities.
